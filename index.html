Your nervous system, listening back.

Before you begin, here is exactly how Mindwave treats your information — in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

  • Everything you enter — HRV readings, stress scores, sleep quality, session history — is stored only on this device, in your browser's local storage.
  • Nothing is uploaded, synced, or shared. There is no server and no account.
  • You can view, export, or permanently delete all of your data at any time from the Progress tab.
  • If you later choose to enable AI weekly insights, your summarised weekly numbers (never your identity) are sent to Anthropic's Claude API using a key you provide. This is off by default.
  • Mindwave is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose or treat any condition.
Your rights under the Privacy Act 2020

Because your data never leaves this device, you hold it directly. Clearing the app's data (Progress → Delete all data, or clearing browser site data) removes everything. Under IPP 6 and 7 you have the right to access and correct personal information — here, you can do both instantly yourself.
